# Icebound Outpost: The Frozen Trade Hub
## Overview
A harsh, snow-covered industrial outpost. The outer coastal rim is dirt, but the main ring road loops twice around the island because it is dangerous—parts of it run through snow, then plunge into long, winding curves completely filled with snow. Alongside this risky main route there are smarter, more advanced roads built for skilled drivers and faster cars. The island survives by mining iron ore from the snowy interior, tapping gas seeps on the rim, and processing everything into steel, fuel, glass and lime for export. It is poor and dependent, constantly importing food, parts and luxuries while exporting processed goods to the main map.

---
### 🌍 Phase I: Local Resource Extraction
The island's value comes from iron ore hidden under snow in the interior plus accessible coastal resources on the dirt rim.

*   **Resource 1: Iron Ore**: Mined from snow-covered sites in the northern interior. *Location:* Northern Snow Fields (drill sites).
*   **Resource 2: Natural Gas**: Vents at the southern dirt rim. *Location:* Southern Gas Seeps (dirt rim).
*   **Resource 3: Silica Gravel**: Found along the exposed coastal dirt. *Location:* Coastal Strip (dirt rim).
*   **Resource 4: Limestone**: Rocky outcrops on the western dirt shore. *Location:* Western Rocky Shore (dirt rim).

*Note: The interior is deep snow. The main ring road is long and dangerous, looping twice with long snow-filled curves. Separate advanced rally-style roads exist for skilled drivers.*

---
### ⚙️ Phase II: Processing (The Dirt Rim Industry)
Raw materials are processed locally on the dirt rim to create tradeable goods. All facilities sit on the exposed dirt for access and road connection.

*   **From Iron Ore**: Smelted into **Steel Ingots** and **Iron Rebar**. *Facility:* Forge & Smelter (dirt rim, west).
*   **From Natural Gas**: Bottled as **Fuel Canisters** for export. *Facility:* Gas Compression Station (dirt rim, south).
*   **From Silica Gravel**: Washed and processed into **Glass Sheets**. *Facility:* Glassworks (dirt rim, east).
*   **From Limestone**: Burned into **Quicklime** for cement and soil amendment. *Facility:* Lime Kiln (dirt rim, south).

*All industry lives on the dirt rim. The long, dangerous ring road connects everything.*

---
### 📦 Phase III: Export (Paying for Survival)
The island's processed goods travel to the main map in exchange for everything it cannot grow or make.

*   **Primary Exports:**
    - **Steel Ingots** → Main Map factories (construction, vehicle manufacturing)
    - **Fuel Canisters** → Main Map gas stations and workshops
    - **Glass Sheets** → Main Map construction and vehicle windows
    - **Quicklime** → Main Map cement plants and farms (soil amendment)
*   **Export Route:** Dirt rim facilities → Loading Dock → Boat/Ferry to Main Map

---
### 🛒 Phase IV: Import (The Lifeline)
The island cannot grow food. Everything organic, plus machinery and luxuries, must be purchased through exports.

*   **Primary Imports:**
    - **Foodstuffs**: Bread, rice, canned meat, vegetables (main map farms)
    - **Parts & Machinery**: Drill bits, engine components, lubricant (main map factories)
    - **Construction Materials**: Standard lumber, concrete mix, rebar (main map bulk yards)
    - **Luxury Goods**: Cigarettes, coffee, alcohol, warm clothing (main map shops)
    - **Chemicals**: Flux agents, solvents for processing (main map chemical plants)

*   **Import Route:** Main Map → Boat/Ferry → Loading Dock → Central Warehouse → Distribution

---
### 🔄 Cargo Flow Summary: The Vital Exchange
**Outbound (Island → Main Map):**
1.  Northern Ice Fields → Drill → Iron Ore → Forge & Smelter → Steel Ingots → Loading Dock → Main Map
2.  Southern Gas Seeps → Compression → Fuel Canisters → Loading Dock → Main Map Gas Stations
3.  Coastal Strip → Glassworks → Glass Sheets → Loading Dock → Main Map Construction
4.  Western Shore → Lime Kiln → Quicklime → Loading Dock → Main Map Farms/Cement Plants

**Inbound (Main Map → Island):**
1.  Main Map Farms → Food Shipment → Island Food Warehouse → Residential Zones
2.  Main Map Factories → Parts Shipment → Island Maintenance Shop
3.  Main Map Shops → Goods Shipment → Island Retail Store
4.  Main Map Bulk Yards → Materials Shipment → Island Construction Sites
5.  Main Map Chemical Plants → Chemicals Shipment → Island Processing Plants

**Key Insight:** The island is poor. Profit margins are thin. One bad season—one collapsed export price, one missed shipment—and the outpost struggles. This creates constant, high-stakes gameplay around maintaining the cargo flow.

---
### 🗺️ Terrain & Roads: The Dangerous Double Loop
The island has two distinct road systems:

**Main Ring Road (Dangerous)**
- Long dirt road that loops around the island roughly twice for safety
- Starts on the dirt rim, then runs through snow for long stretches
- Plunges into many tight, snow-filled curves and switchbacks
- Slow, risky, and punishing — the "normal" route for trucks and daily traffic

**Advanced Rally Roads (Smart Drivers)**
- Separate, more technical roads built for skilled drivers and faster cars
- Tighter corners, bigger elevation changes, narrower snow paths
- Meant for experienced players who want to go faster or take shortcuts
- Connect key points (drill sites, forge, docks) in smarter, riskier ways

**Terrain Summary**
- Outer rim: dirt, where all facilities and housing sit
- Interior: deep snow with iron ore drill sites
- Southern dirt rim: gas seeps, docks, warehouse, main processing
- Roads are the only way to move cargo — no shortcuts through open snow

The island is built for dangerous, high-skill driving. One mistake on the long snowy curves and you lose the load.
